ABOUT GLOBALCOT

The activity of the Company is widely extended all over the world. We have active positions in all the cotton regions of Central Asia. Our activity is expressed through active trade, storing, financing of cotton farms and processing of seed cotton.

Capacity

The Company has a capacity for storing about 10 thousand tons of cotton fiber, each year we sale approximately 30-40 thousand tons of cotton fiber. Logistics has a particular position in our business for timely and reliable fulfillment of the orders of the Customers. Our arrangements help the Customers to feel extremely comfortable. We always draw attention to a special attitude to each of the Customers in common. Quality control of supplied cotton fiber has a special priority for the Company. Globalcot supports cotton growers and at the same time put efforts to increase the efficiency of the farms in order to supply competitive and quality goods to world market. We have a capability to sale, supply cotton through our head offices in the United Arab Emirates, Russia and other representatives and agencies which are located and perform their activities in more than 30 countries of the world. Staff of Globalcot through constant trips all over the world set new and close connections with both growers and consumers of cotton fiber. Regular survey of the market and participation in International Cotton Conferences help our experts in defining a correct strategy for the supply of the Customers with the Goods. Optimism and expertise of the staff are directed to a constant improvement of work methods and enhancement of cooperation with the Customers both in growing and use of cotton fiber. We always take care about our Customers.

Our History

History of the Company starts in 1990. According to the experts, Globalcot is very perspective Company. The company was established as a result of an amalgamation of two companies, Progress one and Globalcot. In the result of such amalgamation the Company extended its activity from local to global scopes. In 1997 the Company acquired control package of shares of TV Service Media Group. The same year the Company purchased several warehouses for storing and further dispatching of the Goods. Thus the logistics activity of the Company has started here. Within this period, Globalcot Company has gained a good reputation in Central Asian cotton region and fiber consuming countries, namely China, Turkey, Russia, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Belarus, and some EU countries. Our goal is to make the history of the Company richer and older. We appeal to our Customers to make a contribution and actively participate in our History. We believe that the history of Globalcot Company will be immemorial.
